# LinkedIn Optimizer Skill

This skill is designed for a one-time structural rewrite of the user's LinkedIn profile to attract AI, Tech, and Healthcare Informatics recruiters.

## Optimization Framework

1. **The Headline**:
   - Avoid "Physician" as the leading word.
   - Format: `[Target Role] | [Unique Medical Value] | [Tech Skill]`
   - Example: `Clinical AI Operations Manager | MD | Prompt Engineering & Medical Automation`

2. **The About Section**:
   - Write in the first person.
   - Tell the transition story: Why did a doctor learn n8n and prompt engineering?
   - Focus on outcomes: Emphasize the 5% claim denial rate drop achievable only by combining clinical knowledge with AI.

3. **The Featured Section**:
   - Recommend visual proof of work. 
   - Specifically: Screenshots or diagrams of the WhatsApp AI Chatbot workflow.

4. **Network Building**:
   - Connect with: "Director of Clinical Informatics", "VP of Health AI", "Medical Director of Product".
   - Avoid connecting blindly with standard recruiters; target hiring managers directly.

## Execution
To generate a complete, personalized Markdown guide with section-by-section rewrites to apply to LinkedIn:
```bash
python run.py linkedin
```
